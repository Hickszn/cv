Home Baker and Consumer of Foccacia.

Just Me eating Focaccia

Hailing from Durham, NC, Zana is a home baker who makes too much focaccia. Sometimes she uses cherry tomatoes as toppings, vegan cheese, rosemary, thyme, or even roasted vegetables. She enjoys slapping that dough against the table and tending after the glutenous mass that eventually becomes savory pillowy foccacia.
